SPORTS QUOTA

The Aim of the sports quota is to financially support sportsmen in the country so that they get proper resources to represent themselves and their teams in various sports. Students who have records of excellence in sports National/State/District level can apply for Undergraduate and Postgraduate degree programmes in Engineering through sports quota admission – Vel Tech for the Academic year 2021. The purpose of the sports quota is to recognize and give weight age to the sports eminence of the candidates and hence marks for sports achievements alone will be considered in ranking the candidates. Interested candidates (both men and women) are requested to report Vel Tech campus along with testimonials and sports certificates (State/National/International) for the Sports Quota Selection trials. (Selection trials’ dates to be announced during the admission season).

Candidates are required to bring their original certificates for verification and one set of photocopy for the following:

  1. Sports Achievement Certificates
  2. Educational Qualification Xth, HSC or any equivalent Examination
  3. Transfer Certificate
  4. Testimonial Certificate
  5. Medical Certificate
  6. Permanent residence proof
  7. Three passport size photos

Candidates must follow the Rules and General Conditions as Follows:

  1. The Scholarship scheme is applicable for the academic year
  2. The Scholarship benefits will be renewed for the consecutive year based on their performance.
  3. The candidate is required to submit their testimonial certificate at the selection trials.
  4. Open/Invitation tournament may be considered
  5. Practice sessions are mandatory for students selected under Sports Quota, if the candidate is irregular to the practice sessions, the scholarship benefits will be terminated.
  6. Candidates are requested to bring their own sports kit for the selection trials.
