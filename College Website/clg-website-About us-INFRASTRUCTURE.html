INFRASTUCTURE


In Vel Tech University the State-of-the-art Infrastructure spread over 100 acres, 22 lakh sq meters built up area providing the environment for perpetual learning. Vel Tech offers campus security, accommodation for staff and students, temples for worship, recreational meet–up points, dining, sports facilities, medical centres, library, transportation, smart classrooms, active learning halls with international standard, well equipped seminar halls, modern conference halls, auditoriums, laboratories, research park, knowledge resource centre, mentoring & counselling cell, training and placement division, entrepreneur development cell, wi-fi, industry institute interaction cell, bureau of higher studies, hostels boys & girls, bank with ATMs, canteen/cafeteria, international guest house, 24×7 Power back-up facility, sewage treatment plants, mineral water plant and post office.
