FAQ For Incoming Exchange Students


The application can be submitted either online or offline. The application must be processed only through the Home University International Office.
In case of online Application, the submitted application to be printed and to be signed by the International Office executive before sending to dint@veltech.edu.in


Exchange students from Partner University need not pay any Tuition Fee. The students have to Pay for only accommodation and foodfor their stay at Vel Tech Hostel.


No stipend or Scholarship will be provided unless otherwise, it is indicated specifically and confirmed in advance.


No, the student shall be allotted for courses works and / or project work offered by any one department. But, the Exchange student may give the preferences of the department and it is advised to select the department that best fits with the student major.


No, Soft copy of Applicationform may be sent along with supporting documents through exchange coordinator / international office at home university by email.


The notification for the admission decision will be sent by email to the applicant and home university international office. Original Admission letter will be sent to Home International Office for Visa purposes, if required.


For the shared accommodation (2 in 1 AC Room) with a double bed, the cost is 150 Euros/Month and for Single accommodation (Separate AC room), the cost is 250 Euro/Month. The above charges covers airport transfers, Indian Food, a/c accommodation, Wi-Fi, access to Gym and transport between the accommodation and university.


The living expenses shall be paid on arrival in INR (Indian Rupees) to the University account.


Vel Tech International Office will arrange authorized agent to visit campus for money exchange with minimum of 200 Euro. Alternatively, the currency exchange agent contact details may be collected from International Office.
Conversion at airport or through any other agent may also be done, but it may incur additional conversion charges


On your arrival, Foreigner Regional Registration Offices (FRRO) procedures have to be completed and it is responsibility of the Foreigner to complete the process. Directorate of International will assist for all these process.
Form C (within 24 Hours of your arrival)
Form S (within a week of your arrival)
Registration in e- FRRO Portal (If Visa Validity is more than 180 days or if Visa endorsed with Registration required within 14 days of Arrival).
The procedures will be taken care of by our Directorate of International Relations in your presence.


Yes, User id and password for the access to Wi-Fi at Hostel will be shared by Hostel In charge on your arrival. Details on campus Wi-Fi will be shared by our Teammate on your arrival at campus


Our Teammate will assist in getting a SIM card on completion of FRRO Procedures. A valid passport copy and photo are mandatory for getting a SIM card.


Table Tennis, Gym, Common Washing Machine (Laundry Facility), Wi-Fi, AC Room Equipped with TV, Fridge and Water Heater.
