Freshman Induction cum Acquaintance (ICA) Programme

The 1st Year of Engineering is initiated with Induction cum Acquaintance (IcA) program. As the name itself implies, this program helps the students in the transition period between +2 and B.Tech in a smooth manner. IcA program aims at making the students know about the applications of basic scientific principles for solving the issues related to Engineering; Conceive new idea, Design and make prototype of object for specific application; Implement and Operate the prototype

The various schools of Vel Tech namely School of Computing (SoC), School of Electrical & Communication (SoEC) and School of Mechanical & Construction (SoMC) have composed a variety of activities to facilitate the Freshman Engineering Students to get acquainted with B.Tech like Technical English, Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Physics, Engineering Chemistry and MATLAB. The program also includes activities of hands on experience like MATLAB, playing with spreadsheet, Build my computer, Hands on 3D printing, my first project (CDIO), power point game,minor project on electronic spreadsheet& PPT, my game through C and my MATLAB project. In addition, there are a variety of career building activities like International Relations, Research & Development, Industry Relation, Corporate Talk, Corporate Tour, Online Learning Resources MOOC, NPTEL etc.

Activities like Corporate Talk and Corporate Touraim at achieving relevant learning, making engineers practice on a personal and informal basis, improving study skills, providing exposure to the best industry practices and the latest technologies.
